WHO WE ARE




About Us


It all starts with my beloved father’s idea. And It is to setup a small Xerox shop with Photo Copy Machine and a Laminator. We start proving digital xerox and lamination of documents to our customers. Meanwhile we face the urge of customer’s need which is more than xerox. So we setup printers and laptops then begin printing documents and photos as well. With scanner inbuilt in printer we now scan and make digital copy of physical documents in almost all the formats including jpeg, png and pdf. Not just that we also setup PVC printer and now we are good to go making smart card of any legal documents.

The journey has to go long. We face issue and complains that most of the photo studio takes huge amount of time and money to photographs and this problem is solved by introducing Photo Studio in our net café. Now we can capture photos using DSLR technologies and print to the most glossy and luxury photo paper. This gives positive response from our customers eventually giving a need for another digital service.

The next thing we realize that people and citizen have to unwillingly roam the city for small digital services like PAN, Aadhar, Voter Correction, Passport and Ayushman Bharat. Considering this issue we convert our citizen’s digital pain into a digital portal that is now called CSC (Common Services Center). But getting CSC is not an easy task. In fact our experts have to pass through series of tests by government authority and then we are given license of CSC. With CSC we become the first VLE (Village Level entrepreneur) in our locality. Now we are open to many government services that we can provide to our citizens.

Rail ticket is something that passengers wait for hours in railway station for booking ticket. So We realize that we need to get them booked their rail ticket in no time. But with individual account on IRCTC we can not issue more than 5 tickets in a month and using someone’s else IRCTC account (even your own) is illegal for making business. But our experts figure it out in no time. And soon we become the authorized Agent with IRCTC. With IRCTC partner, we are free to book as many tickets as we want excluding tatkal.

One day a bidder contacts us. He is extremely afraid of losing his tender in coal India because of tender closing time. This brings us another branch of HDS which is now called E-Tendering Section in Hindustan Digital Seva. With this seva started, we can help contractors how to fill online e-tender in almost all government e procurement system including coal India, IREPS, HERL and Nagar Nigam (Jharkhand Tenders). The biggest problems our engineers face is the unsupportable issue of java plugin in chrome browser. But our engineers make such software environment that we can now help fill any e-tender our customer wants. We are now helping contractors filling online e-tender but many bidders still face the issue of getting digital signature, MSME, stamp paper and contractor license. Hindustan Digital Seva contacted e-mudra and become the partner of e-mudra. With e-mudra we are now ready to make digital signature of any class with all types of certificates (signing as well as signing & encryption) issued by CA. A great thank to e-mudra for proving us digital signature certificates as well as tokens in affordable price. This makes the e-tender section so smart that the problems of getting e-stamp paper and contractor license is no longer an issue.

Money wire is something that now an individual has to wait in queue for hours in bank for the transaction. Keeping that thing in mind, we decide to make online transaction for our customers. But using simple saving account will not allow us to do productive job. Here comes mobisafar. We apply the partnership to mobisafar and now we can do all kind of AEPS transaction including sending money, receiving money, balance enquire in any branch without actually visiting the bank. Special thank to SBI for proving us bhim aadhar. With BHIM Aadhar money wire is now made simple and easy.

Onwards and Upwards, pf and pension are amount that is sometimes difficult for an employee to withdraw. While receiving so many requests and suggestions we decide to help any employee across the nation working in any company or organization to help them withdraw their pension and pf to his/ her bank account. Services like KYC, change in name, correction of date of birth and many more are now made simple.

Note 1: we are not the partner of EPFO. We only help our customers understand about EPFO and how to withdraw pf+pension in bank account.

Note 2 : Please never ever share your OTP, UAN, password with anyone. If you have problem understanding your UAN related stuffs then visit our center. You will know all things.

Making website and web application are something that is common in IT industry. How can we forget this service too? In fact we have a team of highly qualified Web Developer, Node Developer and .NET Developer for making any kind of website one wants. If you are running a business no matter how small it is you will need a website to grow your business online as well as offline. So don’t hesitate to call us if you need either a static website or a dynamic website or a web application. We have all kinds of web services.

Not just that we are also leading in software solutions. We get almost dozens of requests across the nation that their PC is running slow, problem in installing a new piece of software and reset laptop to its original factory position etc. So, Software Solution is a branch of HDS which aims at providing software Sevas online and offline. You can either connect to anydesk or visit personally our company for the required service.

Last but not the least. Today almost every company faces data breach and data leak. That’s why we have very special team to handle this kind of vulnerabilities. No matter how your drive is formatted, you can get almost 90% of your permanently deleted data including personal photos, videos and music back to its original state. You can even retrieve your data if your C driver is formatted irrespective of any operating system. In the worst case your pc might be dead, but still there is possibility to get your important data back. And the very last thing we want to share is to secure your children, family and friends by spying on them. Get your loved one’s live location, phone call logs, text messages, audio and Video whey they are Out.
